Corn Ribs

All about Corn Ribs

This technique for making corn ribs will make you forget everything you know about eating corn on the cobâ€”and will, without a doubt, become your favorite way of making corn.

Ingredients

  • Corn
  • Chili Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Ground Cumin

Steps

  1. Stir together salt, chili powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, and cayenne in a small bowl; set aside.
  2. Drizzle oil over corn; sprinkle on about 2/3 of spice mixture. Toss well, being sure to smear spices and oil evenly over each rib.
  3. To finish indoors, preheat the oven's broiler and set a rack 7 to 8 inches from the heating element. Line a baking pan with foil.
  4. Remove and brush with melted butter; season to taste with remaining spice mixture.
