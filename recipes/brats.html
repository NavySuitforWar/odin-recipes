Brats

All about Brats

Brats are a staple in Wisconsin culture. They are delicious and are perfect for Packers games. But they must be cooked the right way in order to fully enjoy them. Let's learn how to make brats now!

Ingredients

  • 1 Pack of Brats
  • 1 Pack of Brat Buns
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 can of Miller Lite

Steps

  1. Cut up the onion into slices.
  2. Add the can of Miller Lite and the cut up onions into a pot. Turn the pot onto low on the stove.
  3. Put the brats in the beer and onions on the stove.
  4. Let the brats cook in the beer and onion mixture for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Turn your grill on medium high heat
  6. Remove brats from beer and onion and grill until charred and perfect
  7. Put in buns and enjoy!
